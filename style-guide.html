FAQS
05
Do you work on renovations or only new builds?

We do both. In fact, we often find that renovations offer unique opportunities to blend existing character with modern, sustainable design. Whether it's reimagining a dated space or extending a structure with minimal impact, we bring the same precision and philosophy to every project—new or old.

02
Do you offer both design and construction services?

Yes, we offer both design and construction services. This allows us to manage your project from start to finish, ensuring a smooth transition from the planning phase to the final build. Our team works collaboratively to maintain consistency, quality, and clear communication throughout the entire process.

03
What types of projects does Terravia specialize in?

Terravia specializes in a wide range of projects, including residential, commercial, and mixed-use developments. We’re particularly experienced in modern, functional design that balances aesthetic appeal with practical solutions. Whether it's private homes, office spaces, retail environments, or large-scale developments.

04
How does Terravia incorporate sustainability into its designs?

At Terravia, sustainability is a core part of our design approach. We prioritize energy-efficient solutions, eco-friendly materials, and smart building practices to reduce environmental impact while enhancing long-term value for our clients.
