FAQS
05
Do you handle both new construction and renovation ceiling works?

We handle both new construction and renovation ceiling projects. Our team regularly works on existing buildings, upgrading interiors with gypsum and grid ceilings while maintaining structural safety, clean finishes, and minimal disruption, delivering the same professional standards for renovated spaces and newly constructed properties alike.

02
What types of ceiling services does New Diamond Interior provide?

New Diamond Interior specializes primarily in gypsum ceiling and grid ceiling work for residential and commercial interiors. We also undertake gypsum partitions, cement board partitions, POP cornice work, PVC ceilings, and complete ceiling finishing services, ensuring durable materials, precise installation, and long-lasting interior performance.

03
How much time does gypsum ceiling installation usually take?

Project timelines vary based on ceiling design, area size, and site conditions. Most gypsum ceiling installations are completed within a few working days. Our experienced team plans each stage efficiently to ensure timely completion without compromising quality, finishing accuracy, or overall workmanship standards throughout the project.

04
Do you offer site visits and cost estimation before starting work?

Yes, we provide site inspections to understand client requirements and site conditions clearly. Based on measurements, material selection, and design complexity, we share transparent cost estimates, helping clients make informed decisions before starting gypsum ceiling or grid ceiling work with complete confidence.
